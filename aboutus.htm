The staff at Picture Perfect all have one thing in common. Promise of Perfection.

We are old fashioned country people who care about our clients, and want to do the best we can for them whatever the occasion.

We have helped out numerous charities over the past years of business and offer an extended help to the future.

 

We do not provide set price lists nor do we have set packages. We provide a one hour free consult (by appointment only) to discuss your needs and requirements. From this we will issue an obligation free quote to the client. This way you only pay for what you require instead of the whole package.

If a client wants to proceed they pay a deposit which locks in an order for them for the date provided to ensure stock and our availability.
